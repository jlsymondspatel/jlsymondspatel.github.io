Site Guide

What Is This Site For?

This site is utlimately an archive for all of my written works, enabling me to showcase them so that others might find them useful or interesting in some way.

How Is This Site Organised?

This site has these main areas:

  • Poetry
  • Writings
  • Engineering
  • Programming
  • Other
  • About
  • Portfolio

All of these areas are governed by the level hierarchy/naming convention below:

  • Areas
    • Sections
      • Topic Series
        • Entries

Within the area of Poetry, each poem is considered as an entry. And so, each poem serves as the smallest unit of content in this area.

The sections within the Writings area each contain topic series, each of which holds a series of entries. Each entry is a single informal essay. All topic series and entries are appended in chronological order: - Writings: - Philosophy and Religion - World - Miscellany

The area of Engineering contains any engineering projects, many of which are related to computer technology, but don't quite fit into the area of Programming below. This are does of course also include projects that are directly related to engineering itself.

The area of Programming contains the programming languages which I have learnt, and wish to learn, as sections. Some sections may not have content within them since I have either not learnt the language yet, or have not had the time to carry out projects within it.

As the name suggests, all sections within the area of Other are completely random, and such that they do not fit into any of the other areas (as a poem, essay, engineering project, or programming project).

The About area is a little information on my background and my interests.

The Portfolio area is a dedicated space for me to showcase projects I think are most noteworthy.

The Nature of My Work

I would just like to add a couple of words here about the nature of, and my intentions for, the work which I share on this site. I essentially just wish to create works that serve a good use, towards a good and True end. This is important to say, because if my words do not serve the purpose I have just outlined, then they exist purely out of my ignorance and error, hopefully to be corrected in the future. I also hope that these works may be enjoyable to read.

Note on Past Works

I would just like to make a final note in that many things that I have written may be outdated and contain ideas/views which I currently do not hold, and also may simply be badly written. I keep them here to be thorough, in the hope that they may even be useful somehow, though not in the way I intended. For clarity, I have labelled such works which I deem inaccurate or outdated, but there may be some which slip through the cracks, and so I would just like to make that point clear here.

The reason I have chosen the system of Topic Series and Entries, is precisely so that I may preserve even those words of mine which I look back on as being wrong, so that my mistakes might not be made again.
