What Do I Do?

In my spare time, I enjoy exploring several areas:

Language Learning

(Learning human languages)

  • Japanese (this has been the language I've been learning the longest)
  • Latin (I'm currently learning this alongside Japanese in the hope of reading old texts)
  • Gujarati (since I'm half Indian (Gujarati), I am currently learning how to read and write it at least, whilst looking at the Grammar)

Programming

(Learning programming languages)

  • Haskell (I wrote my dissertation in this language, and have enjoyed the most and am quite familiar with, however it has been some time since I used Haskell)
  • Assembly (a recent interest of mine since I've been looking at low-level programming and how computers work)
    • BASH (I've been learning scripting to learn how to automate things, but also as a general tool when interfacing with UNIX-based computers)

Engineering

(Building stuff)

  • Electronics (since I've been looking into how computers work I want to get the basics right, and so I'm exploring how they work fundamentally)
  • Radio (I do some casual radio DXing on a shortwave radio, so learning how radios work with the ARRL Handbook is something I dabble in)
  • GitHub Projects (building things with programming languages in order to build projects, or to learn languages themselves.):
    • https://github.com/jlsymondspatel
    • https://github.com/WyrBox/

Arts

(Writing and creating other things of more general meaning)

  • Essays (I write essays after some thought on things I find interesting in the hope that I might offer some additional insight)
  • Poems (I enjoy writing poems in order to convey some meaning I find important)
  • Audiobooks (when I have the time, I enjoy recording various audiobooks)
  • Stone Carving (I enjoy engraving stone/bricks with hand chisels, again, only when I have the time)
  • Calligraphy (same again, when I have the time)

The Nature of My Work

I would just like to add a couple of words here about the nature of, and my intentions for, the work which I share on this site. I essentially just wish to create works that serve a good use, towards a good and True end. This is important to say, because if my words do not serve the purpose I have just outlined, then they exist purely out of my ignorance and error, hopefully to be corrected in the future. I also hope that these works may be enjoyable to read.

Note on Past Works

I would just like to make a final note in that many things that I have written (in my Writings) may be outdated and contain ideas/views which I currently do not hold, and also may simply be badly written. I keep them here to be thorough, in the hope that they may even be useful somehow, though not in the way I intended. For clarity, I have labelled such works which I deem inaccurate or outdated, but there may be some which slip through the cracks, and so I would just like to make that point clear here.

The reason I have chosen the system of Topic Series and Entries in Writings, is precisely so that I may preserve even those words of mine which I look back on as being wrong, so that my mistakes might not be made again.
